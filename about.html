What Is This?

"The Female Football Fanatic" is a podcast site that focuses on football while giving voice to an underrepresented segment of the fan population--women. Though I do talk a little basketball from time to time, the main topic of conversation revolves around the best sport on the planet--college football--with a side of NFL talk.

Who Is This?

"The Female Football Fanatic" was invented and created by Ren Simon, the voice behind the podcasts. Ren considers herself a "Southern traitor" as a born-and-raised Southerner who is a Big Ten fan and a Michigan alum. Previously a sportswriter for various sites and blogs such as Bleacher Report, Diehard College Football Fans, WolverinesMix.com, etc, Ren is working on building this project from the ground up.

Open Volunteer Positions

"The Female Football Fanatic" would love to have guests on her podcasts, as well as co-hosts. As giving voice to female sports fans is one of the site's goals, I'd love to have an interested, knowledgeable and passionate woman co-host or guest. However, anyone with strong football knowledge is welcome to submit audio clips for review, and we will discuss how to collaborate on a podcast.
